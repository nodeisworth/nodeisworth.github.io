So lets make our first example for communication between the product service and the bill service using the work queues

How it will work :

  1. Our product service will connect to our rabbitmq server and will have its own queue in which it will enqueue messages ( in this case product name) and theses messages will dequeued from the queue by the bill service to generate bills for them 
  2. Our bill service will connect to our rabbitmq server and will have its own queue in which in will enqueue messages ( in this case bills for products passed before in the product queue) and theses messages will be dequeued from the queue by the product service   
  3. So we can say that our product service will enqueue product names in the product queue and then product names will be dequeued from the queue by the bill service and then it will generate bills for them and then it will enqueue them in the bill queue and then product service will dequeue them and send them to the client


Messages Acknowlegment : 

When service receive a message from the queue and processed it it acknowledge the message or tell the rabbitmq server that I proceed this message so delete it from the queue and thats for better memory use


Durable Queue : 

When a service create a queue it passes an options called durable and if its value is true so queue will live even after rabbitmq restarts and if its false it will be lost after rabbitmq restart
